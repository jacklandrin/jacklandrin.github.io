Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the OnlyBaby iOS app (“OnlyBaby”, “we”, “us”) handles your information.

Effective date: 2026-01-30 Applies to: OnlyBaby iOS app

Summary

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not run third‑party ads or analytics SDKs in the app.
  • Your data is stored in app files on your device, and may sync via iCloud Documents if you are signed into iCloud and iCloud Drive is available.
  • You can export your data (for example as JSON) using iOS share features; you control where it is sent.

Information you provide

OnlyBaby is designed for personal tracking. The app can store information you enter, such as:

  • Contraction session entries (start/end time, duration).
  • Baby log entries (diaper events and milk feeding volume in mL).
  • Optional baby birthday (to calculate age).

How your data is stored

OnlyBaby saves your information to files used by the app (for example, JSON files).

  • On-device storage: saved locally on your device.
  • iCloud Documents (optional): if iCloud is available on your device, OnlyBaby stores these files in your iCloud Documents container so they can sync across your Apple devices. Apple’s iCloud services are governed by Apple’s terms and privacy policy.

Sharing / exporting

OnlyBaby includes export features (for example, sharing JSON backups). When you choose to export or share, iOS will ask you where to send the data (AirDrop, Messages, Mail, Files, etc.). The recipients and services you choose may process your data according to their own privacy policies.

Network access

OnlyBaby does not require an account and does not include built-in advertising or third‑party analytics. If you use iCloud Documents, data synchronization is performed by iCloud.

Children’s privacy

OnlyBaby is intended for use by parents/guardians and caregivers to track personal routines. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data retention and deletion

Your data remains on your device and/or in your iCloud Documents container until you delete it in the app, uninstall the app, or remove the files from iCloud Drive / your device.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and update the effective date above.
